Introduction

At DolphyTech ("we", "us", "our"), we build Remembra, an open-source AI memory layer. We believe in transparency about how we handle data—especially since we're building tools that help AI systems remember things.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our website (remembra.dev), documentation (docs.remembra.dev), and cloud services.

Information We Collect

Website Visitors

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Basic analytics data (pages visited, time on site, referral source)
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address (anonymized for analytics)
  • Cookies for site functionality (see Cookies section)

Cloud Service Users

If you use Remembra Cloud, we collect:

  • Account information: Email address, name, company (optional)
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe (we don't store card numbers)
  • Usage data: API calls, storage usage, feature usage for billing and optimization
  • Memory data: The memories, entities, and relationships you choose to store

Contact & Support

When you contact us, we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Any information you provide in your message
  • Support ticket history

Self-Hosted Deployments

If you self-host Remembra, we collect absolutely nothing.

The software runs entirely on your infrastructure. Your memories, entities, API keys, and all other data stay on your servers. We have no access to it, no visibility into it, and no way to retrieve it. That's the whole point of self-hosting.

The only time we might know you're using Remembra is if you choose to contact us for support or participate in our community.

How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Provide our services: Run the Remembra Cloud platform and process your requests
  • Process payments: Manage subscriptions and billing through Stripe
  • Improve our products: Understand how features are used and identify areas for improvement
  • Communicate with you: Respond to support requests, send important service updates
  • Ensure security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with law: Meet legal obligations when required

What We Don't Do

  • We don't sell your personal data to anyone
  • We don't use your stored memories to train AI models
  • We don't share your data with third parties for marketing
  • We don't access your stored memories unless required for support (with your permission) or legal compliance

Data Security

We take security seriously. Our cloud infrastructure includes:

  • Encryption in transit: All connections use TLS 1.3
  • Encryption at rest: Data stored with AES-256 encryption
  • Access controls: Strict internal access policies and audit logging
  • Regular audits: Periodic security reviews and penetration testing
  • Secure infrastructure: Hosted on reputable cloud providers with SOC 2 compliance

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a security breach affecting your data, we will notify you promptly as required by applicable law.

Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate (e.g., Stripe for payments, hosting providers). They're bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business transfers: If we're acquired or merged, your data may transfer to the new entity (we'll notify you)

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Correction

Update inaccurate or incomplete data

Deletion

Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")

Portability

Export your data in a machine-readable format

Restriction

Limit how we process your data

Objection

Object to certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at admin@dolphytech.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (authentication, preferences)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (can be disabled)

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we'll delete your data within 30 days, except where we're legally required to retain it (e.g., financial records for tax purposes).

Children's Privacy

Remembra is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have, please contact us and we'll delete it promptly.

International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States. If you're accessing from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the US. We use standard contractual clauses and other safeguards to ensure adequate protection for international transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. For major changes, we may also send an email notification.
