Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between self-hosted and Pro Cloud?

Self-hosted runs on your own servers — you manage everything. Pro Cloud is fully managed by us — just sign up and start using the API. Same features, different ops burden.

Can I switch from self-hosted to Pro Cloud?

Yes! Use our import/export feature to migrate your memories. We provide migration guides in the docs.

What happens if I exceed my limits?

We'll notify you before you hit limits. If you exceed them, API calls will return a 429 error. You can upgrade anytime from your dashboard.

How do I cancel?

Cancel anytime from your dashboard. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. You can export all your data before canceling.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not happy, contact us within 14 days of subscribing for a full refund.
